# Code of Conduct

FLUI follows a simple standard: be direct, technical, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Keep discussion focused on evidence, tradeoffs, and project goals.
- Challenge designs and implementations, not people.
- Assume maintainers and contributors are working in good faith.
- Make room for disagreement without harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Respect privacy; do not publish private messages, security details, or
  personal information without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Sexualized attention or imagery in project spaces.
- Publishing private information or security exploit details without consent.
- Bad-faith participation intended to derail, intimidate, or exhaust others.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate comments, close threads, block users, or restrict
participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors. Reports can
be made through GitHub moderation tools or by contacting a maintainer privately.
